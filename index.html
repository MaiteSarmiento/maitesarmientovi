Maria Teresa Sarmiento

PhD Candidate in Economics

University of Texas at Austin

Maria is a PhD Candidate in Economics at UT Austin. Her research explores how income inequality affects corporate financing, the liquidity premium, and macro-financial stability using general equilibrium models with heterogeneous agents.
